Something Familiar

Island Bites - $$

Sink your teeth into the mouthwatering delights at Island Bites, Taniti's premier burger haven, where each bite is a symphony of flavors featuring locally sourced ingredients and a tropical twist on the classic burger experience.

SkyFeathers - $

Indulge in a wing-lover's paradise at SkyFeathers, Taniti's cherished wing joint, where crispy perfection meets exotic sauces, elevating the island's culinary scene with every saucy, finger-licking bite.

Sunrise Flavors - $

Start your day right at Sunrise Flavors, Taniti's beloved breakfast diner, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the tempting scent of tropical fruits, offering a delightful morning haven for hearty breakfasts and island-inspired morning rituals.

Something Different

Thai Tropics - $$

Embark on a culinary voyage to Thailand at Thai Tropics, Taniti's authentic Thai restaurant, where the fragrant spices of Southeast Asia infuse each dish with a burst of flavor, creating an unforgettable dining experience that transports you straight to the heart of Thailand's vibrant street food scene.

Sakura Symphony - $$$

Elevate your sushi experience at Sakura Symphony, Taniti's upscale sushi haven, where masterful chefs craft exquisite rolls from the freshest ocean catches, and the elegant ambiance transports diners into a world where precision meets creativity for a truly refined dining affair.
