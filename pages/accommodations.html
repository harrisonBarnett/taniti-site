A 4-Star Experience

Nestled along the pristine shores of Taniti, The Clamshell Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury and comfort, earning its esteemed status as a 4-star retreat in this tropical haven. The architecture, reminiscent of elegant clamshells, seamlessly blends with the natural surroundings, offering a harmonious fusion of modern sophistication and island charm.


With spacious, well-appointed rooms boasting breathtaking ocean views, guests at The Clamshell are treated to a sanctuary of relaxation. Indulge in culinary delights at the on-site restaurants, unwind by the azure pool, or rejuvenate body and soul at the spa.

Taniti's hostels embody the spirit of communal adventure, providing budget-conscious travelers with a welcoming and vibrant atmosphere. Whether you choose a beachside dorm or a cozy village enclave, these hostels offer a unique blend of affordability and social connectivity.
